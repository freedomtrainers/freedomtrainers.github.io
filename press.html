About Election Day: Our Civic Holiday

Election Day: Our Civic Holiday is a nonpartisan civic initiative calling on Americans to treat Election Day as a day of community action — voting, helping neighbors vote, staffing polling places, observing the count, and making the day a celebration of democratic participation. The initiative is not affiliated with any political party, candidate, or campaign. It draws on research from the Hands Off Our Vote messaging project and the Election Protection Training Playbook. More information at election.holiday.
